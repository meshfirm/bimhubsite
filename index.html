Frequently Asked Questions

Why use BIMHub?

BIMHub facilitates a collaborative design process and transparent communication in a design project. Its' centralize information management ensure that you find the right information at the right time in the right place for the right task.

Why use a digital tool?

BIMHub allows your project team work remotely and access the information from distributed locations. It ensures that everybody in the project team are on the same page, and no information or decision is lost.

Who is BIMHub for?

By civil engineers, for civil engineers. Our founders worked on large civil engineering projects and were frustrated by the project management tools. They created BIMHub to offer a more enjoyable experience and better alternative for project managers, designers and BIM coordinators.

How much will BIMHub save me?

With BIMHub, you manage your projects scope, budget and schedule with one tool. It decrease the number of tools needed and enables you to deliver high quality, on time and budget.
